## Practical Notes
- June = rainy season (tsuyu) in Japan — expect humidity and rain, especially in Tokyo
- Mt. Fuji views from Hakone may be obscured by clouds in June (common)
- Indoor activities (arcades, museums, shopping) work well on rainy days
